Hi, I'm Timo – the creator of Simplify Yourself

I believe life is best experienced with intention and adventure! Whether I’m exploring remote corners of the Americas or helping people live more mindfully, I’m all about discovering what truly matters and sharing those insights with others.

“Live a minimalist life, improve the lives of others and do more of what you love!”

Our Mission

At Simplify Yourself, I believe that life should be intentional, not overwhelming. Since launching this blog in August 2015, my goal has been to share practical insights, minimalism principles, and travel experiences to help you live with more clarity, purpose, and freedom.

My Journey

2015

The Beginning

Launched "simplify yourself" blog on August 4th with my first post "Blogstart". Started exploring minimalism principles and sharing insights about intentional living.

2016-2017

Epic Americas Adventure

Embarked on a life-changing 14-month journey through 11 countries across North and South America. From New York to Patagonia, this adventure deepened my minimalism philosophy and inspired countless blog posts. During this time, I learned Portuguese while traveling through Brazil and other Portuguese-speaking regions. Created travel blog "hinreisend" (later lost to hacking).

2017-2018

Integration & Growth

Returned to Berlin and integrated travel experiences with established minimalism practice. Continued developing content that bridges adventure, technology, and intentional living.

2018-2025

Community Building

Expanded the blog's reach, experimented with technology (3D printing, brain stimulation), and built a community around mindful living. Established "Simplify Yourself" as a trusted voice in the minimalism space over 7+ years of continuous growth.
